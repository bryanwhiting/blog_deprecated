Product development and predictions

 Mar 3, 2017
What do products have in common with prediction models? They both solve problems, depend heavily on the samples in which they’re trained, and should adapt as samples grow. In modeling: Bias: the mean difference between your estimate and the average of the truth. Variance: the average squared difference from the truth. In product development: Bias: the distance between how your product solves a problem and how the “ideal” solution would look like Variance: How each person’s experience is with the product, and how well it solves the problem for them.
